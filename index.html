What is 3D printing

it's a process which builds a three-dimensional object from a CAD model, usually by successively adding material layer by layer

Right now because of Corona Virus 3D printers all around the world are printing stuff hospitals need, like face shields and respirators
But before pandemic 3D printing was used to other stuff for example to build houses
It can even be used for more destructive thigs
It's possible to print guns in your own house
Thankfully it's hard to do since print need to be coated with special chemicals
But what do I print with?
there are many types of filament to print with:
-PLA
-ABS
-PET
-FLEX
-WOOD and Metal
every one is good for other purpose and has other properties like heat resistance or material strength
